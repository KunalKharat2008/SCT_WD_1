Frequently Asked Questions

Are they compatible with Android and iOS?

+

Yes, the Aura Pro connects seamlessly via Bluetooth 5.3 to any modern operating system, with full app support on both Apple App Store and Google Play.

Can I wear them in the shower or while swimming?

+

The Aura Pro is IPX7 rated, meaning it can survive submersion in up to 1 meter of water for 30 minutes. However, Bluetooth signals do not travel well underwater, so swimming is not recommended.

What happens if I lose one earbud?

+

You can use the 'Find My Aura' feature in our app to play a loud sound or track its last known GPS location. Replacement single earbuds are also available in our store.
